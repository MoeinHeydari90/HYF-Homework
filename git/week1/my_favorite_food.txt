Ghormeh sabzi is a traditional Persian dish known for its rich flavors and aromatic herbs. Here's a basic recipe:



Ingredients:

1 lb (450g) stew beef, cut into cubes
2 cups chopped fresh herbs (parsley, cilantro, green onions)
1 cup dried fenugreek leaves (optional)
1 large onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 dried limes (limoo amani), pierced
1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
1 teaspoon turmeric
Salt and pepper to taste
Vegetable oil for frying



Instructions:

Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown.
Add garlic and beef cubes. Cook until beef is browned on all sides.
Stir in turmeric, salt, and pepper.
Add chopped herbs and dried fenugreek leaves. Cook for a few minutes until herbs are wilted.
Pour in enough water to cover the ingredients. Add dried limes.
Cover and simmer over low heat for 2-3 hours, stirring occasionally, until beef is tender.
Add kidney beans and cook for an additional 30 minutes.
Adjust seasoning if needed and serve hot with steamed rice.
